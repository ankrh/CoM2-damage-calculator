# Master of Magic — Spell List by Realm and Rarity

Compiled from the Fandom wiki realm pages in `Manuals/MoM source - Fandom site/`.
The five colored realms each have 10 spells per rarity tier (40 per realm, 200 total). Arcane is a shared realm available to all wizards; it is tiered but has fewer spells per tier.

---

## Arcane

Available to all wizards regardless of spellbook selection.

### Common
- Dispel Magic
- Magic Spirit
- Spell of Return (free to every wizard)
- Summoning Circle

### Uncommon
- Detect Magic
- Disenchant Area
- Enchant Item
- Recall Hero
- Summon Hero

### Rare
- Awareness
- Create Artifact
- Disjunction
- Summon Champion

### Very Rare
- Spell of Mastery
