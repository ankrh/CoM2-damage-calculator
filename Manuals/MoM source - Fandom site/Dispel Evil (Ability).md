[Ability DispelEvil](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/3/37/Ability_DispelEvil.png/revision/latest?cb=20120225195524)

**Dispel Evil** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. With this ability, a unit can make a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that has a good chance of killing enemy [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** and [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** creatures outright. It is particularly potent in banishing the [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead").

Only one unit in the game possesses **Dispel Evil** by default: the [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel"). It is not possible to add the **Dispel Evil** ability to any unit that does not already possess it, though it is possible to add a [Dispel Evil Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_Damage "Dispel Evil Damage") component to a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")'s [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") by equipping that Hero with a "Holy Avenger" weapon.

Although the in-game tooltip for this ability claims that it
affects an entire enemy unit, this is actually not the case. In reality,
the ability only works on a single [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** per attack, unlike the *[Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil")* spell. Furthermore, it only imposes a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-9 Save Penalty** on created [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") (same as the spell), as opposed to the -10 stated in the tooltip.

## Description

The god of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life Realm**
is benevolent, but is not without its share of wrath. In particular, it
is very wrathful against the "abominations" originating in the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** and [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos**
realms. To this end, this god has imbued some of its followers with the
ability to banish creatures from those realms back whence they came. **Dispel Evil** disrupts the magic that keeps these creatures in the primary [Planes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Plane "Plane"), sending them back to their home realms if they cannot properly resist the effect.

## Effect

Dispel Evil is automatically used whenever the unit engages in close combat - whether voluntarily making a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") against a target or [Counter Attacking](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") to an enemy assault.

When used, it forces the target unit to make one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the source unit. To resolve this effect, the game rolls a random number between **1** and **10**. This is then compared to the target's current [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** **-4 (-9 if [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead"))**. If the rolled number is higher than the modified Resistance score, the target unit is struck for [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") [Irreversible Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points#Irreversible_Damage_Points "Damage Points") equal to the full [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** of one of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**, slaying one figure instantly and possibly inflicting further [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** to the next figure in line.

#### Timing within the Melee Attack

:   As a [Melee Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack#Melee_Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), Dispel Evil is executed simultaneously with the unit's [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"); thus, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** destroyed by a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks** or Ability FirstStrike [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike")
    will not deliver Dispel Evil, and figures destroyed by Dispel Evil will
    still deliver damage unless the unit is attacking and has Ability FirstStrike [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike").

#### Immunity

:   Units that are not [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** or [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** are totally immune to this power, as are units with [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"). They do not need to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, instead completely ignoring the attack.

:   Also, any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** remaining after all factors have been computed, as well as any [Charmed](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Charmed "Charmed") hero, is rendered effectively invulnerable to Dispel Evil by virtue of its resistance score.

## Units with Default Dispel Evil

There is only one unit that possesses **Dispel Evil** by default:

[Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel") [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel")

No other units start the game with a **Dispel Evil** ability - though it can be added to some [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") through the use of [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") (see below).

## Acquiring Dispel Evil

There is no known method of adding the **Dispel Evil** ability to any unit that does not already possess it.

Note however that it is possible to add [Dispel Evil Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_Damage "Dispel Evil Damage") to a Hero's attack by equipping that Hero with a [Magical Melee Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") containing the "[Holy Avenger](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). The result is identical to the **Dispel Evil** ability combined with the [Bless](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bless "Bless") spell, with the exception of [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), which also enable [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") to use **Dispel Evil** on their [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") (the ability is otherwise not available on [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") weapons).
