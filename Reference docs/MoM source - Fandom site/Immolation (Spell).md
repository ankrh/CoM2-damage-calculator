**Immolation** is an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **150** it may be cast on any unit on the overland map to envelop it in flames, granting it the [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") **Immolation** [Unit Ability](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Ability "Unit Ability"). This causes the attacks of the enchanted unit to gain an added effect that is triggered as a [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), and delivers a strength [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **4** [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage") attack. This attack becomes stronger in proportion to the number of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** in the enemy unit.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **30**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Immolation** bestows the unit with the [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") **[Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation")** ability, which, in turn, empowers its attacks with [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage"). The ability is triggered using the rules and conditions governing [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack").

### Immolation

:   *Main article: [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation")*

When a unit possessing **Immolation** makes an attack against an enemy unit, it will perform an extra [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage") attack with a strength of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **4**. This works as an added effect (Touch Attack), and will be triggered by dealing damage with the following attacks: [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") (any type), [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath Attacks**, and [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack").
Most notably, it does not trigger off other Touch Attacks, but it can
trigger multiple times during the melee sequence for units that have one
of the aforementioned [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack").

#### Immolation Damage

:   *Main article: [Area Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Area_Damage "Area Damage")*

:   [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage") is the Area variant of [Fire Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Damage "Fire Damage"), which means it is stronger in proportion to the number of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** in the enemy unit. It is delivered to each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit separately, using its full Attack Strength. In turn, each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** can also make [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls against it separately, using their unit's full [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score, and no [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** can be hit for more than its current [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points**. At the end of the sequence, [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** is summed up and applied to the unit as a whole.

#### Immunities

:   Targets possessing [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") or [Righteousness](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Righteousness "Righteousness") [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Righteousness](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Righteousness "Righteousness")** are completely unaffected by [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage"). Targets with [Fire Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Immunity "Fire Immunity") [Fire Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Immunity "Fire Immunity") defend against it as if they had [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**, and thus will almost certainly not suffer any damage either. In addition, the conditional [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** bonus granted by [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Bless](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bless "Bless")**, [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**[Resist Elements](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resist_Elements "Resist Elements")**, [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**[Elemental Armor](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Elemental_Armor "Elemental Armor")**, and the [Large Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Large_Shield "Large Shield") [Large Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Large_Shield "Large Shield") ability all apply against [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage").

## Usage

**Immolation** may be cast during combat for a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **30**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Immolation** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **150**, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **2** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and is not dispelled by a rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its benefit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Red** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

**Immolation** may be removed outside of combat by its caster
by opening the unit statistics window and clicking on the enchantment's
icon. This will bring up a confirmation dialog for releasing the spell.
The primary reason to do this would be in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** spell, **Immolation** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least one [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is almost never guaranteed.

Customized Wizards possessing [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**11** Spellbooks at game creation may choose **Immolation** as one of their starting spells, in which case it will be available for casting immediately on the first turn.

Wizards who possess at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**8** Spellbooks, or Wizards with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**11** Spellbooks who did not select **Immolation** as a guaranteed spell, will be able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") this spell at some point during their campaign. Wizards with fewer than [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**8** Spellbooks have a random chance of being able to Research it. The chance for this spell to appear increases with the number of [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay.

**Immolation** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **740**.

With at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**1** Spellbook, the **Immolation** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Immolation** is cast on a unit to make it stronger against enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**. Although it may inflict extra damage on enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units** as well, the true potential of this spell is only felt when the unit attacks (or is attacked by) an enemy Multi-Figure unit.

This is because **Immolation** delivers an attack against each and every enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** simultaneously, thus the more figures there are, the more damage the enemy unit may suffer in total. Therefore, units with **Immolation** cast upon them are exceptionally useful for taking on enemy [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), which often contain upwards of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **4 figures**.

Because the [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage") occurs simultaneously with the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage")
during melee combat, it's important to remember that the enchanted unit
is still at risk. Therefore, the spell is best cast on tougher melee
units, who can stand multiple attacks from enemy units. [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure** [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") are often the best targets for this spell.

On the other hand, since **Immolation** is also triggered by both long- and short range attacks ([Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack"), [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack"), [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack"), [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath"), and [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack")), it can provide additional damage to any of these attacks. In particular, units with [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical** ranged attacks and [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath** attacks may make good targets for this enchantment, as these attacks are fairly difficult to enhance otherwise.

In addition, **Immolation** complements Gaze Attacks quite well, as they are both strong against [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-figure** targets, and [Immolation Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation_Damage "Immolation Damage") is [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**-based, allowing these units to exploit any weakness their targets may have before being dealt [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") damage. **Immolation** can also trigger off of a Gaze Attack when defending against a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack").

## Known Bugs

**Immolation** can be considered bugged, since it is triggered with every attack that the corresponding unit makes. This includes Ranged [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magic**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulder** and [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Missile** attacks, even though the attacker does not have to stand next to its victim to perform these.

For [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, and [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), the victim may have to withstand 2 (or more) separate **Immolation**
attacks during a single melee sequence, as all of these attacks will
trigger it in their respective phases. This is, of course, in addition
to it also being triggered in the [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") phase.
