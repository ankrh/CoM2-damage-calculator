An **Undead** unit is a unit that has been killed and reanimated by certain [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** magic spells or powers. The resulting unit is now considered a [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realm. It receives all of the special bonuses and [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities")
that all other Death units possess, but is now also subjected to the
same weaknesses – particularly the inability to heal normally, nor to
gain [Experience](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level").

Only [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Units "Normal Units") and [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Units "Fantastic Units") can be reanimated as **Undead**, and the process is not reversible in any way.

## Granted Abilities

### Modified Upkeep Costs

A [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") turned **Undead** will have its [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") reduced to [Food](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Food "Food")**0** and [Gold](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gold "Gold") **0**. This makes **Undead** Normal armies extremely cheap to maintain.

A [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") will have its [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") multiplied by [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1½**.

### Fantastic Unit

:   *Main article: [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit")*

An **Undead** unit is considered a [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") for most purposes, making it immune to a wide variety of spells and effects that can only target [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Units "Normal Units").

On the other hand, a [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") can be affected by certain other spells that cannot target [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Units "Normal Units").

### Death Unit

A reanimated unit becomes permanently associated with the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death**
realm. This association allows the unit to enjoy benefits of certain
spells, but also makes it vulnerable to a variety of other spells and
effects.

The following spells have special effects on [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** units:

* [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Darkness](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Darkness "Darkness"), [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Cloud of Shadow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cloud_of_Shadow "Cloud of Shadow") and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Eternal Night](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Night "Eternal Night") will grant **Undead** units **+1** to all of [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack")[Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack")[Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack")[Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack")[Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack")[Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath")[Lightning Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lightning_Breath "Lightning Breath")[Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense")[Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance"), as well as a (normally hidden) **+1** to the damage of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack").
* [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[True Light](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/True_Light "True Light") and [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[Heavenly Light](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Heavenly_Light "Heavenly Light") will give **Undead** units **-1** to all of [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack")[Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack")[Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack")[Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack")[Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack")[Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath")[Lightning Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lightning_Breath "Lightning Breath")[Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense")[Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance"), as well as a (normally hidden) **-1** to the damage of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). The **-1** penalty will cause all gaze attacks other than that of a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") to fail automatically.
* [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[Star Fires](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Fires "Star Fires") and [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil") can target the unit. Undead units, specifically, resist [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil") at a whopping [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-9**.
* Units with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[Bless](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bless "Bless") receive [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+3** against attacks made by [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** units.
* [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")[Holy Word](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Word "Holy Word") applies a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-7 Resistance** penalty to Undead units, instead of the normal [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-2**.
* The [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel")'s [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)") ability may harm the unit. Undead units, specifically, resist this ability at a whopping [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-9**.

Furthermore, if a [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") with the [Caster](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Caster "Caster") ability becomes **Undead**, it will now be able to cast spells from the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realm *instead* of its original realm. This does not affect [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero").

### Inability to Gain Experience

**Undead** units do not gain experience over time, nor from winning battles. However they will retain the [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") (and all associated bonuses) that they had at the time when they were reanimated.

Nonetheless, the [Armsmaster](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Armsmaster "Armsmaster") ability still affects them (this is probably a bug).

### Unhealing

An **Undead** unit no
longer heals naturally from turn to turn, like other units do.
Furthermore, it cannot be healed by any spell or abilities other than [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal") and [Regeneration](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Regeneration "Regeneration"). This makes it much more difficult to maintain an **Undead** army over consecutive battles, and requires replacing units altogether as they are "worn out".

The ability of Regeneration to heal **Undead** units and/or restore them after destruction makes this spell/ability extremely potent when combined with Undead units. Thus, [Trolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Trolls "Trolls") make the ideal **Undead** servants.

### [Poison Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Immunity "Poison Immunity") [Poison Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Immunity "Poison Immunity")

An **Undead** unit receives Poison Immunity, which means that it cannot be hurt by [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") from any source. It will automatically succeed in any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll required to avert such damage.

### [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity")

An **Undead** unit receives Illusions Immunity, which provides the following benefits:

* [Illusion Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion_Damage "Illusion Damage") inflicted on this unit does not bypass its armor. The unit gets to make its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls as normal.
* During combat, this unit will spot all enemy [Invisible](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Invisibility "Invisibility")
  creatures regardless of how close they are. Those creatures become
  semi-visible on the screen, and may be targeted freely like any other
  unit.
* This unit is completely immune to many [Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Curse "Curse") from the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** realm. It either may not be targeted by such curses at all, or is simply not affected by them.

### [Cold Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cold_Immunity "Cold Immunity") [Cold Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cold_Immunity "Cold Immunity")

An **Undead** unit receives Cold Immunity, which means that it cannot be affected by any spell or ability that delivers [Cold Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cold_Damage "Cold Damage"). It will simply not receive any [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** from such sources.

### [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity")

An **Undead** unit receives
Death Immunity, which means that it cannot be affected by any spell or
ability that causes death or fear, including most of the combat spells
from the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death Realm**.

## Ways of Creating Undead

There are multiple methods of creating **Undead**, all of which have the same effects on the final unit created:

### Create Undead Damage Points

Any [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee** damage dealt by units with the [Create Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Create_Undead "Create Undead"), as well as any damage dealt by the [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal") ability, the [Vampiric Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Vampiric_(Item_Power) "Vampiric (Item Power)"), or the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Life Drain](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Drain "Life Drain") spell, is considered to be [Create Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Create_Undead "Create Undead") damage. If at least half of the [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points")
which killed a unit were Create Undead, the other side won the battle,
and the other side has space in its army for an additional unit, the
unit will be reanimated after the battle, serving the enemy. Note that,
for units with [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal"), *only* the damage from [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal") counts, and thus [Death Knights](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Knights "Death Knights"), [Demon Lords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Demon_Lord "Demon Lord"), and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") will usually not create undead. Combat summons, units with [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") cannot be made undead in this way.

### Animate Dead

The [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Animate Dead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Animate_Dead "Animate Dead")
spell allows restoring a unit that died in the current battle as an
undead unit. If owned by the opposing wizard, it will be destroyed at
the end of the battle; otherwise it will continue in your army, as a
normal **Undead** unit. Combat summons, units with [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") cannot be made undead in this way.

### Black Channels

The [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")[Black Channels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Channels "Black Channels") spell, in addition to its other effects, turns its target **Undead**. [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") can only be affected by this spell if they are already **Undead**. This is the only way in which a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") can become **Undead**.

## Known Bugs

According to its in-game description (and the article above), the **Undead** trait is supposed to grant a unit 4 distinctive immunities. However, in the official game, only [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") is actually applied. It may seem that this is not the case because [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Black Channels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Channels "Black Channels")** *does* also grant the other three, *but only as long as it is in effect*. The [Unofficial Patch 1.50](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unofficial_Patch_1.50 "Unofficial Patch 1.50") fixes this issue (W144FIX.TXT).
