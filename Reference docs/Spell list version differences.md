### Spell list differences

Compared [Manuals/MoM Spell List.md](Manuals/MoM Spell List.md) against [Manuals/CoM spells.md](Manuals/CoM spells.md). Every realm still has 10 spells per rarity tier; differences below are additions, removals, renames, and tier moves.

#### Arcane
- **Removed**: Recall Hero; Awareness. (Spell of Return no longer explicitly listed but presumably still auto-granted.)
- **Added (new)**: Heroic Heart.
- **Moved in from another realm**: Plane Shift (was Life Uncommon in MoM).

#### Life
- **Removed**: True Light; Planar Seal; Planar Travel; Plane Shift (moved to Arcane); Dispel Evil; Righteousness; Astral Gate; Tranquility.
- **Added (new)**: Exorcise; Sanctify; Altar of Peace; Divine Order; Exaltation; Enlightenment; Call to Arms; Supreme Light. (Life Force is also flagged "new" in CoM but the name also exists in MoM — effect has been redesigned.)
- **Tier moves**: Heavenly Light (Uncommon → Common); Stream of Life (Rare → Uncommon); Inspirations (Very Rare → Rare).

#### Death
- **Removed**: Black Channels; Berserk; Subversion; Black Wind; Cursed Lands; Famine; Cruel Unminding.
- **Added (new)**: Zombies; Blood Lust; Syphon Life; Reaper Slash; Drought; Wave of Despair; Summon Demon.
- **Renamed**: Wrack → Gate of Hades; Word of Death → Annihilate; Death Spell → Massacre; Death Wish → Final Wave; Dark Rituals → Dark Ritual (also tier-moved); Shadow Demons → Shadow Demon.
- **Tier moves**: Wraith Form (Rare → Common); Terror (Common → Rare); Dark Ritual (Common → Rare).

#### Nature
- **Removed**: Giant Strength; Stone Skin; Wall of Stone; Move Fortress; Nature Awareness; Nature's Wrath.
- **Added (new)**: Fairy Dust; Wild Boars; Construct Catapult; Survival Instinct; Seismic Mastery; Fairy Ring.
- **Renamed**: Basilisk → Great Lizard; Path Finding → Land Linking; Ice Storm → Blizzard; Cracks Call → Crack's Call (spelling); Gaia's Blessing → Gaea's Blessing (spelling).
- **Tier moves**: None.

#### Sorcery
- **Removed**: Word of Recall; Enchant Road; Wind Mastery; Suppress Magic; Dispel Magic True; Disenchant True; Disjunction True. (The three "True" dispels were removed entirely — the weaker non-True versions already existed in Arcane and remain there.)
- **Added (new)**: Focus Magic; AEther Sparks; Dispelling Wave; Water Elemental; AEther Binding; Uranus' Blessing; Power Link.
- **Tier moves**: Blur (Uncommon → Common); Counter Magic (Common → Uncommon); Flying Fortress (Very Rare → Rare); Haste (Rare → Very Rare).

#### Chaos
- **Removed**: Eldritch Weapon; Metal Fires.
- **Added (new)**: Mystic Surge; Blazing March.
- **Renamed**: Call Chaos → Apocalypse; Great Wasting → Doomsday.
- **Tier moves**: Flame Blade (Uncommon → Common); Fire Storm (Rare → Uncommon); Doom Bat (Uncommon → Rare); Doom Mastery (Very Rare → Rare); Warp Reality (Rare → Very Rare).

### CoM → CoM2 differences

Compared [Manuals/CoM spells.md](Manuals/CoM spells.md) against [Manuals/CoM2 spells.md](Manuals/CoM2 spells.md). CoM2 no longer enforces exactly 10 spells per rarity tier — several realms expand Rare and Very Rare to 11–12 spells, and Arcane grows to 14. Everything below is the delta from CoM to CoM2.

#### Arcane
- **Added (new)**: Tree of Knowledge (13th slot; casting grants permanent access to Armorer's Guild, Fantastic Stables, and Linking Tower in all cities).
- Count goes from 13 to 14 spells.

#### Nature
- **Added (new)**: Abundance (Rare; boosts max city population); Call the Wild (Rare; rampaging-monster manipulation); Roots of Genesis (Very Rare; converts world tiles to forests and generates power per forest tile); Clairvoyance (Very Rare; world-map reveal + node-countering immunity + Forester on all units — was dropped from Sorcery in CoM, now reappears here).
- **Renamed / split**: Seismic Mastery → Planetary Mastery. In CoM, Seismic Mastery bundled map reveal + node power + free Earthquakes on big casts; in CoM2 the map-reveal effect is split out into the separate Clairvoyance spell, while Planetary Mastery keeps only the node-power + auto-Earthquake effect.
- **Tier moves**: None among carryover spells.

#### Life
- **Added (new)**: Discipline (Common; rank-scaling buff); Destiny (Very Rare; converts a normal unit into a Supernatural Life creature); Phoenix (Very Rare; Regen 3, Raise Dead ×3, Healing Aura, Fire/Death Immunity); Ruler of Heaven (Very Rare; enchantments harder to dispel + bonus religious power).
- **Tier moves**: Endurance (Common → Uncommon); Divine Order (Uncommon → Rare); Holy Arms (Very Rare → Rare).

#### Sorcery
- **Added (new)**: Philosopher's Stone (Uncommon; magical weapons on produced units + combat-start Counter Magic 70 + Monument/Linking Tower boost); Disillusionise (Rare; strips beneficial city enchantments from an enemy city); Reinforce Magic (Rare; +2 resistance and +2 magical ranged strength to all caster's units); Wind Mastery (Very Rare; flying + speed bonus globally — was fully removed in CoM, restored in CoM2).
- **Tier moves**: Spell Blast (Uncommon → Rare); Magic Immunity (Rare → Very Rare).

#### Death
- **Added (new)**: Mislead (Common; Misfortune-based debuff on a normal unit's army); Vampire (Rare; 20-melee flying Life Steal -4 creature, promoted in from elsewhere); Darkest Hour (Very Rare; halves other wizards' religious + research building output, doubles dispel resistance of own city enchantments); Ruler of Underworld (Very Rare; all own units get Wraith Form in combat + blocks magical/mithril/adamantium from piercing weapon immunity).
- **Tier moves**: Mana Leak (Common → Uncommon); Drain Power (Uncommon → Rare).

#### Chaos
- **Added (new)**: Animate Ammo (Common; drains a normal unit's ranged ammo to convert into temporary casting skill — replaces Warp Wood); Fate Mastery (Uncommon; auto-casts 3 Fire Bolts per combat + forces double saves against caster's save-or-suffer spells); Inner Power (Rare; buffs all units that have Fire Immunity or Lightning Resistance); Chain Lightning (Very Rare; strength-40 lightning on the target and two nearest enemy units); Blazing Eyes (Very Rare; grants Doom Gaze 3 to all Chaos creatures, or +1 to existing Doom Gaze).
- **Removed**: Warp Wood (replaced by Animate Ammo at the same slot).
- **Tier moves**: Fire Storm (Uncommon → Rare).
