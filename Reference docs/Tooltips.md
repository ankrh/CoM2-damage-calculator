# Tooltip Suggestions

---

## Abilities group

### Stoning Gaze
For each defender figure: Defender resist at −X or die.
**Immune:** Stoning, Magic.

### Stoning Touch
For each attacker figure: Defender resist at -X or die.
**Immune:** Stoning, Magic.

### Death Gaze
For each defender figure: Defender resist at −X or die.
**Immune:** Death, Magic, Righteousness.

### Life Steal
For each attacker figure: Defender resist and take damage = roll − (resistance - X); attacker heals.
**Immune:** Death, Magic, Righteousness.

### Doom Gaze
Deals X damage.

### Poison Touch
X times for each attacker figure: Defender resist or take 1 dmg.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** No modifier.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Modifier -1.
**Immune:** Poison.

### Hidden Gaze Attack
Physical attack that gazes are attached to.

### Dispel Evil
Chaos/Death defender: For each attacker figure: Defender resist at -4 or die
Undead defender: For each attacker figure: Defender resist at -9 or die
**Immune:** Magic, all other unit types.

### Armor Piercing
Halves defender's defense.

### First Strike
Attacker delivers melee and touch attacks before the defender retaliates. Not when counter-attacking.
**CoM 1:** Only triggers if ≤ 24 damage is needed to kill the top figure.
**Immune:** Negate First Strike

### Negate First Strike
Negates the attacker's First Strike.

### Large Shield
Extra defense vs. ranged, thrown, breath, and gaze attacks.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60** +2.
**CoM 1 & 2:** +3.

### Lucky
+10% To Hit, +10% To Block, +1 Resistance.
**MoM 1.31:** Opponent −10% To Hit (melee, ranged, and breath).

### Illusion
Defender's defense = 0, overriding all immunities.
**Immune:** Illusion, True Sight.

### Long Range
Missile and boulder ranged distance penalty capped at −10% instead of −30%.

### Caster
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** Haste does not double this unit's magical ranged attack.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Qualifies unit for Supreme Light bonuses.

### Doom Damage
All attacks skip to-hit and defense rolls; attack strength = exact damage dealt.

### Fire Immunity
Immunity to fire breath and Immolation.
**CoM 2:** Also qualifies unit for Inner Power bonuses.

### Cold Immunity
Immunity to cold damage attacks.

### Death Immunity
Immunity to Death Gaze, Life Stealing, and Cause Fear.

### Poison Immunity
Immunity to Poison Touch.

### Stoning Immunity
Immunity to Stoning Gaze and Stoning Touch.

### Supernatural
Attacker's hits always deal at least a minimum damage, regardless of the defender's blocks.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** No effect.
**CoM 1:** Min = (hits − 5) / 2.
**CoM 2:** Min = hits / 3.

### Weapon Immunity
Extra defense against non-magical attacks from normal units.
**MoM 1.31:** Defense = 10. Bug: Thrown attacks bypass this.
**MoM 1.60:** Defense = 10.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Defense +8.

### Lightning Resist
Cancels the Armor Piercing component of incoming Lightning Breath.
**CoM 2:** Also qualifies unit for Inner Power bonuses.

## Abilities/Enchantments group

### Resistance to All
+X to resistance.

### Holy Bonus
+X to melee attack, defense, and resistance.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Also +X to ranged/thrown/breath attack.

### Illusion Imm./True Sight
Immune to Illusion and Invisibility.
**MoM 1.31 bug:** Illusion Immunity is checked on the defender instead of the attacker for Blur.

### Cause Fear / Cloak of Fear
For each defender figure: Resist or freeze for the melee exchange.
No resistance modifier.
**Immune:** Death, Magic, Righteousness.
**MoM 1.31 bug 1:** Defender's Cause Fear never fires — only the attacker's does.
**MoM 1.31 bug 2:** Each of the defender's failed rolls also fears one attacker figure (self-fear), bypassing immunity.

### Invisible
Ranged attackers cannot target this unit.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** Opponent −10% To Hit on all To Hit rolls (melee, ranged, thrown, breath, gaze).
**CoM 1 & 2:** 20% chance to negate each incoming hit before defense rolls; stacks with the Blur enchantment up to a 30% cap.
**Immune:** Illusion.

### Magic Immunity
Immunity to magic ranged attacks, Immolation damage, Wall of Fire, Death Gaze, Life Steal, Cause Fear, Stoning, and Dispel Evil.

### Undead
Unit becomes a fantastic death creature. Grants Death, Cold, Poison, and Illusion Immunity.
**MoM 1.31 bug:** Only Death Immunity applies; Cold, Poison, and Illusion Immunity are missing.

### Missile Imm. / Guardian Wind
Immunity to ranged missile attacks.

### Immolation
Fire area attack hitting all defender figures each combat phase.
**MoM 1.31:** Strength 4. Bug: Also fires alongside ranged attacks.
**MoM 1.60:** Strength 4.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Strength 10.
**Immune:** Fire Immunity, Magic Immunity, Righteousness.

## Global conditions

### True Light / Darkness
**True Light (MoM 1.31 & 1.60 only):** Life fantastic units +1 mel/rng/def/res; Death units −1.
**Darkness:** Death fantastic units +1 mel/rng/def/res; Life units −1.
**Neither/Both:** Neither is cast, or both cancel out.

### City Walls
Defense bonus applied to the defender only.
**MoM 1.31:** Intact city wall: +3 def. Destroyed city wall or inner area: +1 def
**MoM 1.60, CoM 1 & 2:** Intact city wall or inner area: +3 def. Destroyed city wall: +1 def

### Node Aura
Matching fantastic units get +2 melee, ranged, thrown, breath, gaze, defense, and resistance.

### Wall of Fire
Area fire attack on all attacker figures, firing before the melee phase.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** Strength 5.
**CoM 1 & 2:** Strength 10.
**Immune:** Fire Immunity, Magic Immunity, Righteousness.

### Warp Reality
All non-Chaos units get −20% To Hit.

### Ranged Attack (checkbox)
Sets combat mode to ranged. Only available if the attacker has a ranged attack.

### Ranged Attack Distance
Missile and boulder only. Magic ranged ignores distance.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** −10/−20/−30% at 3/6/9 tiles.
**CoM 1:** −10/−20/−30% at 4/8/12 tiles.
**CoM 2:** −10% at 4 tiles, then −3% per additional tile.
Long Range caps penalty at −10%.

## Enchantments group

### Prayer / High Prayer
**Prayer:** All units in combat gain +10% To Hit, +10% To Defend, +1 resistance.
**High Prayer:** All units in combat gain +10% To Hit, +10% To Defend, +2 defense, +3 resistance.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60, CoM 1:** Also +3 melee.
**CoM 2:** Also +2 melee.

### Chaos Channels
Unit permanently becomes a fantastic Chaos creature.
**+Defense:** +3 defense. **MoM 1.31 bug:** Applied twice in combat (net +6).
**+Fire Breath:** **MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** Strength 2. **CoM 1 & 2:** Strength 4.
**+Flight:** Flight has no consequences in this calculator.

### Resist Elements / Elemental Armor
**Resist Elements:** Bonus defense and resistance vs. magical ranged attacks, breath attacks, Stoning Touch and Stoning Gaze.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** +3 bonus.
**CoM 1 & 2:** +4 bonus.
**Elemental Armor:**
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** +10 defense against Chaos/Nature conventional damage (ranged magical, fire breath, immolation, etc.). +10 resistance against Stoning Touch and Stoning Gaze.
**CoM 1 & 2:** +12 defense against magical ranged attacks and breath attacks.

### Bless
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** +3 defense and +3 resistance vs. breath attacks, Death Gaze, Life Steal, Cause Fear and Chaos/Death creatures' melee and ranged.
**CoM 1 & 2:** +5 defense and +5 resistance vs. breath attacks, Death Gaze, Life Steal and Cause Fear.

### Invulnerability
Grants Weapon Immunity. Reduces damage by 2 per defense roll.
Does not reduce Doom damage.

### Holy Weapon / Holy Arms
+10% To Hit on melee, thrown, missile, and boulder attacks. Negates Weapon Immunity.
**MoM 1.31 bug:** Does not affect thrown attacks.

### Animate Dead
Unit becomes undead (fantastic Death creature): gains Death, Cold, Poison, and Illusion Immunity; +1 attack, +1 defense, +10% To Hit, and Weapon Immunity.
**MoM 1.31 bug:** Only Death Immunity applies; Cold, Poison, and Illusion Immunity are missing.

### Flame Blade
Bypasses Weapon Immunity.
**MoM 1.31 & 1.60:** +2 melee, thrown and missile attack.
**CoM 1 & 2:** +3 melee and +2 missile.

### Shatter
All attack strengths reduced to 1.
